The Subtletization of Marriage and Mourning Customs in Shanghai

The subtletization of marriage and mourning customs in Shanghai reflects the city's unique fusion of traditional Chinese values and modern urban culture. As a cosmopolitan hub, Shanghai has seen its traditional customs evolve in response to globalization, social progress, and the influence of Western values.
